How to find nutrition values for ingridients !!!

To calculate nutrition values you need to finde the nutrition data on the different ingredients. You can find these on the back of the product package or for raw vegetables, on the different nutrition data internet pages, ex: nutritiondata.self.com

Nutritional calculations You need the following information to calculate kiloCalorie value (kCal):
Sum up the total amounts of carbonhydrates in grams and multiply by 4,1 Sum up the total amounts of Protein in grams and multiply by 4,1 Sum up the total amounts of Fat in grams and multiply by 9,1
